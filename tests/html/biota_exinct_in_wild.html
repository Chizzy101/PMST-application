Species Profile

Top

About the Pedder Galaxias

The Pedder Galaxias is a small, slender freshwater fish, with gold iridescence often present on the back and sides. This species is no longer found in its natural habitat, though was once endemic to Lake Pedder in southwestern Tasmania. There are translocated populations at Strathgordon Dam and Lake Oberon, both locations are free from the threat of alien fish (brown trout) species. This species spawns in spring and lives to at least six years.
